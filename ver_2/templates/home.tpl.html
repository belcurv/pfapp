Welcome to pfapp!

pfapp (Personal Finance App) is a small collection of utilities for calculations related to personal finance. pfapp currently consists of the following utilities:

FIRE Calculator

Calulations related to Financial Independence / Early Retirement.

Commute Calculator

How much is your commute costing you?

Net Worth Tracker

Track your net workth over time. How does your actual net worth match the projections from the FIRE Calculator?

Portfolio

A (very) simple tracker, designed to calculate the aggregate returns of a collection of stock and bond mutual funds. Also includes portfolio reallocation suggestion based on the target returns calculated in the FIRE Calculator.
